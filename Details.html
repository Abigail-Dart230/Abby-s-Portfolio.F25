Quiet Seasons



Date of Creation: October 18, 2025
Date of Completion: October 19, 2025

This artwork is from ‘Quiet Seasons’, a story I’ve been developing over the past year. It follows Miki, whose quiet and uninterpreted routine begins to shift when a loud, persistent classmate takes an unexpected interest in her. This story is a slow-burn, slice-of-life tale about friendship, growing pains, and the subtle ways people change each other.
